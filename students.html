Students

Research Students Supervised by Prof. Roshan G. Ragel

Prof. Roshan G. Ragel supervises postgraduate research students pursuing PhD, MPhil, and MSc degrees at the University of Peradeniya and in collaboration with other institutions, as well as undergraduate students in their final year research projects. Research areas span Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Bioinformatics, Computer Vision, Wearable Computing, and Engineering Education.
